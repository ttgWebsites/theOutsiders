Interesting Details

  • S.E. Hinton began writing the novel at the age of 15, and it was published by the time she was 18.
  • Though the novel is set in Tulsa, Oklahoma it is never stated as such in the novel.
  • In 1983 a film version of the novel was made. Many of the cast members, though rather unknown at the time, went on to be extremely successful. Some of whom include Tom Cruise, Emilio Estevez, Rob Lowe, Patrick Swayze, and Diane Lane.
